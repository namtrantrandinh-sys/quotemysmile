## Description (4000 chars)

Your dream smile, in your hand.

QuoteMySmile is the fastest way to compare indicative dental quotes from
AHPRA-registered Australian dentists near you — without an awkward phone
call, without a clinic visit just to ask for a price.

How it works
1. Tell us what you need — checkup, whitening, fillings, crowns, or
   something cosmetic.
2. Take four guided photos of your teeth in 60 seconds. The on-camera
   guides show you exactly how to frame each one.
3. We share your photos and your radius with verified dentists nearby.
4. Watch their indicative quotes arrive live, on your screen.
5. Pick the one that suits — price, distance, availability, reviews.
6. Secure your booking with a refundable platform deposit. Final fees
   are confirmed at your clinical exam, paid directly to the dentist.

What makes it different
· Live, on-screen quoting — not "call us for a price"
· Open feed — every quote is visible, dentists compete
· Strict revision policy — one requote per dentist
· AHPRA-only — every quoting practitioner is registered
· Australian — designed for Australian patients and clinics
· Your data — photos and location are deleted 30 days after each request

Privacy & safety
We never track in the background. We never sell location or health data.
Photos are stored privately, visible only to matched dentists. Card data
is handled by Stripe — we never see your card number.

Final clinical fees and treatment decisions remain between you and your
chosen dentist. QuoteMySmile is an introduction marketplace, not a
clinical service.
