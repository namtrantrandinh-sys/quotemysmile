## Full description (4000 chars)

Your dream smile, in your hand.

QuoteMySmile is the fastest way to compare indicative dental quotes from
AHPRA-registered Australian dentists near you — without an awkward phone
call, without a clinic visit just to ask for a price.

How it works
1. Tell us what you need.
2. Take four guided photos in 60 seconds.
3. Verified dentists nearby send live quotes to your screen.
4. Pick the one that suits.
5. Secure with a refundable platform deposit.

What makes it different
· Live OPEN feed — every quote is visible to you
· One revision per dentist — clean, fair competition
· AHPRA-only practitioners
· Photos and location are deleted 30 days after each request
· Apple Pay & Google Pay supported

Privacy
We never track in the background. We never sell location or health data.
Card data is handled by Stripe — we never see your card number.

Indicative quotes are confirmed at your clinical exam. Treatment decisions
and final fees remain between you and your chosen dentist. QuoteMySmile is
an introduction marketplace, not a clinical service.

## Data safety form answers

Data collected
- Location (Precise) — Required, App functionality, Encrypted in transit, Optional delete
- Photos — Required, App functionality, Encrypted in transit, User can request delete
- Personal info: Name, Email, Phone — Required, App functionality + Authentication
- Financial info: Payment info — Optional (only when booking), App functionality, Encrypted in transit
- App activity: App interactions — Optional, App functionality + Analytics
- Crash logs (Sentry) — Optional, App functionality + Analytics

Data sharing
- Not shared with any third party.
- Payments are processed by Stripe (their own data handler agreement).
- Push tokens flow through Expo Push Service.

Data security
- Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+).
- Data is encrypted at rest (Supabase / GCP).
- Users can request their data be deleted via support@quotemysmile.com.au.
- App has been independently security-tested.
