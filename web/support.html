Support

We reply within one business day.

Patients

Booking changes, deposit refunds, questions about a quote, or anything else patient-side: support@quotemysmile.com.au. Include your booking number (visible on the booking detail screen) so we can help faster.

Dentists

AHPRA verification queries, payouts, or to add a new practitioner to an existing clinic account: clinics@quotemysmile.com.au. New clinic applications: /for-dentists.

Privacy & data requests

For a copy of your data, correction requests, or to delete your account from outside the app: privacy@quotemysmile.com.au. The in-app path is Settings → Delete account.

Clinical safety

If you have an urgent clinical concern, contact your dentist directly or call 000. We're not a clinical service and can't triage emergencies.
