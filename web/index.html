Real quotes, not estimates.

Every quote on QuoteMySmile is the work of a named AHPRA-registered dentist personally reviewing your four guided photos. We call them "indicative" because regulation requires it — in practice, they're tight enough that most patients book and pay almost exactly what they saw.

  • AHPRA-verified at the source. Every quoting dentist is checked against the AHPRA public register before they can send a quote. Suspended or restricted practitioners are silently dropped.
  • Named, personally-signed quotes. The dentist's full name, AHPRA number, and clinic are on every quote card. They acknowledge responsibility for the figure at the moment they send it. The record is timestamped and audited.
  • One revision per dentist. Clean competition, no death-spirals. The dentist's first quote is their best foot forward.
  • Deposits held by us, returned in full. Refundable platform security deposit, Airbnb-style. Held by QuoteMySmile, never the clinic, and fully returned to your card within 5 business days of your visit.
  • Photos & location deleted after 30 days. Never tracked in the background. Never sold. Auto-purged 30 days after each request closes.
  • No fee-splitting with dentists. AHPRA-compliant by design. Your treatment fee flows directly from you to your dentist — we never take a cut of it.

Real questions.

How accurate are the quotes really?

Each quote is the work of a real, named, AHPRA-registered dentist personally reviewing your four guided photos. They commit their name and registration number to it. "Indicative" is the legal label — in practice the final fee at the chair-side is usually within a few percent of the quote. Clearer photos = tighter quotes.

What does it cost me?

Nothing. Free to use. Booking a consult places a refundable platform deposit ($50–$100) on your card, which is returned in full when you attend. The dentist pays us a flat A$5 per attended booking — that's our only revenue.

What if I cancel or no-show?

Cancel 24h+ before the slot → full refund. Cancel inside the window or no-show → deposit forfeited (the clinic time is wasted; the no-show fee is the trade).

Which areas are you live in?

Launching in inner-east Melbourne first — Camberwell, Hawthorn, Glen Iris, Surrey Hills. Other Australian metros follow as dentists join.

What if my dentist isn't on the platform?

Forward them to /for-dentists — free to join, A$5 only on completed visits, in under an hour.

What happens to my photos?

Stored privately, visible only to dentists matched by AHPRA registration + geofence. Auto-deleted 30 days after the request closes. See privacy.

Is this a medical service?

No. QuoteMySmile is an introduction marketplace. Every clinical decision and final fee stays between you and your chosen dentist.
