FOR AHPRA-REGISTERED PRACTITIONERS

Quote on your terms.

Set your radius, pick the work you take, and respond to live patient requests from the chair-side. Live indicative quoting, one revision, no awkward phone tag — and no fee-splitting with us.

AHPRA-only. We verify against the public register before activating you.

What you get.

  • Live, geofenced requests. Patients within your chosen radius (2–30 km) only. No tyre-kickers in Perth when you're in Hawthorn.
  • One revision per quote. No marathon negotiations. Strategic, not a death-spiral.
  • AHPRA shield baked in. Compliance filter screens every note for testimonial language, outcome promises, and guarantees before the patient sees it.
  • Self-pause anytime. Settings → toggle off. You silently drop out of the broadcast until you toggle back on.
  • Emergency premium toggle. Up to +50% for after-hours / same-day. Transparent to the patient — they see the uplift on the quote card.
  • No fee-splitting. Treatment fees flow patient ↔ dentist directly. We don't take a cut of your clinical work.

PRICING · ONE FLAT FEE

Free to join.

A$5  per attended booking

No monthly fee. No per-quote charge. No take-rate on treatment fees. We only get paid when a patient actually visits you — A$5 flat. Pay on a single monthly invoice.

How we verify you.

  • AHPRA public register check. Real registration number, real name, real division. Automatic on signup, re-checked daily. Suspensions remove you immediately.
  • ABN verification. Active business via the ABR register. Sole-practitioner, partnership, or company — all fine.
  • Professional Indemnity Insurance. You upload your current PII certificate at signup. Re-verified annually.
  • Clinic geofence. You're matched only to patients within your service radius. You set the radius. Pause it anytime.
  • Immutable audit log. Every quote, requote, and acknowledgement is timestamped and stored for AHPRA / regulator review. Your defence record is built-in.
